ACM-W

Supporting, celebrating and advocating for Women in Computing

Purpose

"ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women." -ACM-W National

What we do

Throughout the year we host several events that are inline with our goal of supporitng Women in Computing. In 2016, we brought Tarah Wheeler author of Women in Tech: The Book to Pullman to talk about her experiences writing the book.

We have also sent our students to the Grace Hopper Conference in Houston, Texas where they network and hear from other women in technology.

If you would like to support us or learn more about us contact Rachel Forbes.

Register for the ACM-W Mentor Program!

In 2015, we introduced the ACM-W mentoring program for women studying computer science. The mentoring program gets our students more connected within the department and helps them throughout their early years in the major.

If you are interested in being a mentor or a mentee please contact Rachel Forbes.
