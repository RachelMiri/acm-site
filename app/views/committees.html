Our Committees

We host several different clubs that help further our mission and provide resources to the local community.

ACM-W Chapter

The ACM-W is a special interest group for Women in Technology. The ACM-W @ WSU supports women who are new to computer science by offering a mentor service and hosting events throughout the year.

Visit the ACM-W.

Computer Security Group

The Computer Security Group within Washington State University’s Voiland College of Engineering and Architecture is the university’s only Registered Student Organization (RSO) dedicated to computer and network security.

Visit the Computer Security Group.

Crimson Code Hackathon

The largest annual hackthon in Eastern Washington, Crimson Code is going into its 6th year. Students show up to create amazing projects together and demo them to sponsors, faculty and their fellow students.

Visit the Crimson Code Hackathon.
